# Setting up your practice assistant, step by step

This takes about 20 to 30 minutes the first time. Almost everything is clicking, and the two times you paste something, this guide spells it out. If any step feels stuck, that is normal computer weather; the notes under each step say what usually helps.

## What you need before you start

- A Windows computer where you can say yes to "Do you want to allow this app to make changes?" (that is called administrator permission).
- Your own Claude account on a paid plan (the regular Pro plan is enough).
- The practice app installer, from the person who set this up for you; they will tell you where to find it on your computer.

## Step 1. Install Claude Desktop

Already have Claude on this computer? Open it, sign in with your own account, and go straight to Step 2.

1. In your web browser, go to claude.ai/download and download Claude for Windows.
2. Open the downloaded file; to find it, look at the bottom of your browser window or in your Downloads folder. When Windows asks "Do you want to allow this app to make changes?", choose Yes.
3. Open Claude and sign in with your own Claude account.

## Step 2. Open Cowork for the first time

1. In Claude, click the Cowork tab.
2. The first time, your computer may need a Windows feature called Virtual Machine Platform turned on. Claude will tell you if so.

Honest note: this is the one step that sometimes needs help. Turning that feature on needs administrator permission and usually a restart, and occasionally a helping hand from the person who set this up for you. A 15-minute screen share solves it in the worst case. Once it is done, it is done forever.

## Step 3. Add the setup helper

1. In the Cowork tab, open Customize in the sidebar, then Plugins.
2. Under Personal plugins, click the plus button, then Add marketplace, then Add from a repository.
3. Paste this address exactly:

   https://github.com/Veeiy/therapy-practice-assistant

4. Click to add it, then find "Practice Assistant Setup" in the list and click Install.

## Step 4. Install your practice app

1. Run the practice app installer you were given; if you are not sure where it is, look at the bottom of your browser window or in your Downloads folder, or ask the person who set this up for you. Say Yes if Windows asks about changes. If Windows shows a blue "Windows protected your PC" message, click "More info", then "Run anyway"; that message appears for new apps that are not yet widely known.
2. Open the practice app once so it can settle in, then close it.

## Step 5. Run your setup interview

1. Back in Claude's Cowork tab, start a new conversation.
2. Say: "Set up my practice assistant."
3. Answer the friendly questions. You can skip any of them. It takes about fifteen minutes, and along the way it will ask you to connect two folders; it tells you exactly what to click.

That is it. When the interview finishes, open your practice app and everything will be arranged the way you described. You can run "set up my practice assistant" again any time you want to change something; it remembers your answers and only asks about what you want to change.

## A short honest note about privacy

Your practice app keeps your real work private on your computer, and its AI help stays off until you ever choose to turn it on. The setup conversation happens in Claude, so it is processed by Anthropic, the company that makes Claude, under your own Claude plan; that is why the interview only ever asks about how your practice runs, never about clients, and why its very first step checks your Claude privacy settings with you.
